About Us

What Are We?

GDG (Google Developer Group) is a passionate community of developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts who share a common goal: to learn, innovate, and grow in the field of technology. We are open to individuals of all skill levels who have a thirst for knowledge and a drive to create.

What We Do?

We organize tech events, workshops, and hackathons to foster innovation and skill development. Through interactive sessions, speaker engagements, and networking opportunities, we empower individuals to enhance their technical expertise, collaborate on projects, and bring creative ideas to life.
