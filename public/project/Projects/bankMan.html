Banking
Management System


By: Kaustubh Pandey

Problem Statement

Banking today involves all the tedious manual processes of maintaining customer accounts, carrier transactions, and all records. This, therefore, leads to a great deal of inefficiency, errors, and delays. To overcome these limitations, the Banking Management System aims to provide an all-encompassing solution for managing the core functionalities of a bank within the digital sphere.

Description

The project was created to address inefficiencies in traditional banking, such as manual processes, errors, and delays. By automating core banking functions, it ensures improved efficiency, accuracy, and a better customer experience. The idea arose from observing limitations in traditional banking and the growing demand for digital solutions. It was inspired by the success of online banking and the need for efficient backend systems.
The Banking Management System is designed to digitize and automate these processes, enabling banks to manage customer accounts, handle transactions, and generate reports more effectively. By integrating Java as the programming language, JDBC for database connectivity, and SQL for database management, this system ensures a user-friendly and secure environment for both administrators and customers. This project draws inspiration from existing banking systems but focuses on creating a streamlined, user-friendly, and secure platform using Java, JDBC, and SQL. The goal was to bridge the gap between customer expectations and the limitations of traditional banking processes.
